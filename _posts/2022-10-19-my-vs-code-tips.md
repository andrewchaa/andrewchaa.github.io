A few tips and tricks I use for the wonderful code editor, VS Code.

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- close a tab: ⌘ W

- close all tabs: ⌘ k ⌘ W

- close other tabs: ⌘ ⌥ T

- show file explorer: ⌘ ⇧ E

- show / hide left pane: ⌘ B

- open a terminal: ^ `

- open a new terminal: ⇧ ^ `

- search files by name: ⌘ P

- search actions by name: ⌘ ⇧ P
